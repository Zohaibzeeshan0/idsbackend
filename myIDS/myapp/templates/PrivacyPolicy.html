Privacy Policy

At DeepThreat, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our intrusion detection system for smart home networks. By using DeepThreat, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

- Personal Information: When you sign up or log in, we collect your username, email address, first name, last name, and hashed password.

- Network Data: DeepThreat monitors network traffic on your smart home devices to detect potential threats. This includes packet metadata (e.g., IP addresses, ports, protocols) but does not include the content of your communications.

- Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our platform, such as login times and feature usage, to enhance user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Authenticate users and provide access to DeepThreat’s features.

- Analyze network traffic to identify and alert you to security threats using machine learning models.

- Improve our services through usage analytics and system performance monitoring.

- Communicate with you regarding account updates or security alerts.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

- Storing personal information securely with encrypted passwords.

- Anonymizing network data where possible to minimize privacy risks.

- Regularly updating our security protocols to address emerging threats.

However, no system is completely secure, and you use DeepThreat at your own risk.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

- With your consent for specific purposes (e.g., sharing anonymized data for research).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us.

- Opt out of non-essential data collection (e.g., usage analytics) where applicable.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of DeepThreat constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact Us.
